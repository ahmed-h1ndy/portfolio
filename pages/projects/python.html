Amazon Product Price Tracker

Lightweight scraper that records Amazon product titles, pricing, and collection dates to monitor price shifts over time.

Focus: Make price monitoring effortless for consumer products.

Features

  • Scrapes product title and price from Amazon product pages.
  • Structures and cleans data for readability before storage.
  • Appends entries to CSV files to build a historical log.
  • Supports automated execution on custom time intervals.

Stack

  • Python 3, BeautifulSoup, and Requests for acquisition.
  • Pandas for data handling and exploration.
  • CSV, OS, Time, and Datetime for scheduling and persistence.
