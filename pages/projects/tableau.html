Customer Engagement Analysis with SQL & Tableau

Challenge: Analyze customer engagement, purchases, and learning behavior using SQL for data exploration and Tableau for visualization.

Action & Tools:

  • Built complex SQL queries with CTEs, views, and analytical functions to understand customer behavior across countries
  • Created reusable views for purchases, ratings, and learning metrics to enable consistent reporting
  • Designed interactive Tableau dashboard with drill-down capabilities for marketing and course strategy insights

Results & Impact:

  • Identified that purchases are concentrated in English-speaking countries, suggesting translation/localization opportunities
  • Discovered India and Nigeria show high engagement but low conversions, indicating potential for regional pricing
  • Found odd months outperform even months due to campaign timing, validating marketing effectiveness
  • Revealed introductory courses dominate, highlighting opportunity for structured progression paths

E-Learning Onboarding Survey Dashboard

Challenge: Understand how students begin their learning journey on an e-learning platform focused on data science education.

Action & Tools:

  • Analyzed onboarding survey data from hundreds of new users to understand demographics and learning preferences
  • Built interactive Tableau dashboard with filtering capabilities by country, subscription status, and learning goals
  • Created visualizations to identify discovery channels, geographic distribution, and skill interest patterns

Results & Impact:

  • Discovered most new users come from USA and India, reflecting strong demand for online data science education
  • Identified YouTube and Google as top discovery channels, informing SEO and content marketing strategy
  • Found data analysis and visualization as most popular learning interests, guiding curriculum development
  • Dashboard enables dynamic filtering for stakeholder decision-making in content strategy and marketing
