Customer Engagement Analysis with SQL & Tableau

Analytical queries and Tableau dashboards that surface how students learn, purchase, and revisit content on a digital platform.

SQL approach: CTEs for modular analytics, reusable views, and analytical queries that feed visual storytelling.

Query highlights

  • CTEs to segment ratings, minutes watched, and purchases.
  • Reusable views for consistent reporting across dashboards.
  • Targeted queries to uncover top purchasing countries and months.

Insights unlocked

  • English-speaking regions dominate purchases, highlighting localization opportunities.
  • High engagement but low conversion in India and Nigeria prompted regional pricing experiments.
  • Odd-month campaigns outperformed even months, confirming marketing pulses.
  • Introductory courses dominated — a chance to build structured progression paths.
