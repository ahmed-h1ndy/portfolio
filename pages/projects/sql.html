Customer Engagement Analysis with SQL & Tableau

Project Overview: This project analyzes customer engagement, purchases, and learning behavior using SQL for data exploration and Tableau for visualization. It demonstrates how to structure queries, build reusable views, and generate actionable business insights.

SQL Highlights:

  • CTEs for modular analysis (ratings, minutes, purchases)
  • Reusable Views (purchases_info, full_student_info)
  • Analytical Queries (top countries, top purchase months)

Key Insights:

  • Purchases are concentrated in English-speaking countries → consider translation/localization to expand
  • India and Nigeria show high engagement but low conversions → potential for regional pricing
  • Odd months outperform even months due to campaign timing → marketing is effective
  • Introductory courses dominate → opportunity for structured progression paths
