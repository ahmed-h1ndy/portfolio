Call Center KPI Dashboard

Project Overview: Analyzing 3 years of call center performance to explore how call volume impacts service performance.

Key Insights:

  • As call volume increases, both waiting time and answer speed rise
  • SLA performance drops consistently at higher volume levels
  • A clear operational challenge: maintaining service quality under heavy load

Metrics Summarized:

  • Total Calls
  • Avg. Answer Speed (sec)
  • Avg. Waiting Time (sec)
  • Avg. SLA (%)
  • Avg. Handling Time (min)
  • Abandoned Calls (count)

Impact: This kind of analysis helps identify capacity limits, staffing needs, and improvement areas to optimize both customer experience and efficiency.
