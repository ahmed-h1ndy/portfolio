YouTube's Top 1,000 Channels

Project Overview: Exploring the rise of YouTube's top 1,000 channels (2004‚Äì2021) with an interactive Power BI dashboard that tracks the evolution of the platform's top creators over time.

Key Insight - Why Was 2014 a Breakout Year?

  • YouTube's algorithm changed significantly in 2012‚Äì2013, shifting from click-based to watch-time-based ranking
  • This led to a massive rise in Let's Plays, vlogs, and long-form content, especially in gaming and commentary
  • Global reach expanded rapidly, with mobile access growing and emerging creators from India, Brazil, and Europe scaling fast
  • Creators who adapted early were rewarded with viral growth and visibility

Another Key Insight - Longevity vs. Dominance:

  • While many top channels were created after 2010, some of the most subscribed were launched in the mid-2000s
  • Early adopters who continuously adapted their content retained massive audiences
  • Newer channels around 2014 showed explosive subscriber growth due to algorithmic maturity and globalization

Call Center KPI Dashboard

Project Overview: Analyzing 3 years of call center performance to explore how call volume impacts service performance.

Key Insights:

  • As call volume increases, both waiting time and answer speed rise
  • SLA performance drops consistently at higher volume levels
  • A clear operational challenge: maintaining service quality under heavy load

Metrics Summarized:

  • Total Calls
  • Avg. Answer Speed (sec)
  • Avg. Waiting Time (sec)
  • Avg. SLA (%)
  • Avg. Handling Time (min)
  • Abandoned Calls (count)

Impact: This kind of analysis helps identify capacity limits, staffing needs, and improvement areas to optimize both customer experience and efficiency.
